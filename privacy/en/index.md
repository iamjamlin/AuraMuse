# Privacy Policy

Updated: June 9, 2026  
Effective: June 9, 2026

Welcome to Auralis.

Auralis is an app centered around white noise, calming music, affirmations, and related companion experiences. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Auralis, and how you can manage your information and permissions.

By using or continuing to use Auralis, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the current version of Auralis and future updates of the app, including features and services provided directly by us.

Please note that some features in Auralis rely on system capabilities or platform services provided by Apple, such as:

- App Store purchases and purchase restoration
- System photo library saving capabilities

These Apple-provided services may process relevant information in accordance with Apple's own privacy policies and service terms. You may also refer to Apple's official privacy information for details.

## 2. Information We Handle

Based on the current features of Auralis, the information we handle mainly includes the following categories:

### 1. Settings and Usage State Created Locally on Your Device

To provide a continuous and stable experience, Auralis may store certain settings and usage states locally on your device, such as:

- Affirmation playback interval settings
- Home screen affirmation font and font size settings
- Dark mode preferences
- Certain playback and display-related states

This information is mainly stored in the app's local sandbox or system local storage on your device, and is used to maintain app functionality. We currently do not use our own account system to upload or store this information on our own servers on a long-term basis.

### 2. Local Content You Choose to Import

If you use the custom audio or custom background features, Auralis may store the audio files, images, videos, and related names that you choose to import locally on your device, so that they can be played, displayed, and managed within the app.

This content is mainly stored in the app's local sandbox on your device. Auralis currently does not upload your imported audio, images, or videos to our own servers, and does not use them for purposes unrelated to playback, background display, or local management.

### 3. Subscription Purchases and Membership Status

When you purchase a subscription, restore purchases, or verify your membership status, Auralis uses StoreKit and App Store capabilities provided by Apple to complete actions such as:

- Loading subscription products
- Confirming purchases
- Restoring purchases
- Checking current membership status

During this process, Apple may process information related to your Apple ID, payment, and transaction verification in accordance with its platform rules. Auralis does not directly obtain sensitive payment credentials such as your bank card number or Apple ID password.

### 4. Audio Resource Downloads and Caching

Some audio content in Auralis may be downloaded and cached locally on your device based on your selections, network conditions, and device status, in order to support playback, pre-caching, and availability in offline or poor network conditions.

These resources are mainly used to:

- Prepare member audio resources when you tap to play them
- Cache member audio when you actively use pre-caching features
- Improve availability and playback experience in offline or weak network conditions

Related caches are mainly stored locally on your device, and may be cleared if you delete the app, if device storage conditions change, or as part of iOS system cache management and resource preparation mechanisms.

### 5. Photo Library Permission and Image Export

When you use export or image-saving features and actively choose to save an image to the system photo library, Auralis may request photo library write permission.

In the current version, Auralis only requests permission to add items to your photo library. This is used to save images that you actively choose to export, and does not allow us to read existing photos in your photo library.

When you import an image or video as a custom background through the system picker, Auralis only accesses the media content you actively select, and does not read other content from your photo library.

## 3. How We Use This Information

We handle the information described above mainly for the following purposes:

- To provide white noise, music, affirmations, and related display features
- To remember your personalized settings and interface preferences
- To support importing, playing, displaying, and managing local custom content
- To determine and synchronize your membership status
- To download, cache, and manage member audio resources
- To support saving exported images to the system photo library
- To help ensure app security, stability, and functionality
- To support troubleshooting, experience improvements, and compliance requirements

## 4. System Permissions We Request

Based on the current features of Auralis, the app may request the following system permission:

### Photo Library Permission

Purpose: To save images that you actively export to the system photo library.  
Scope: The current version requests add-only access, and does not use this permission to read existing content in your photo library.

When you import an image or video as a custom background through the system picker, Auralis only accesses the media content you actively select, and does not read other content from your photo library.

If you deny this permission, you can still use most core features of Auralis, but features related to saving images may not work properly.

## 5. Storage and Retention

### 1. Local Storage

In the current version of Auralis, most settings data, user-imported local content, cache states, and certain feature data are mainly stored locally on your device.

### 2. Audio Resource Cache

Audio caches may be stored on your device and may be affected by factors such as:

- You deleting the app
- iOS system resource management policies
- System cache cleanup or resource re-preparation mechanisms

### 3. Retention Period

We handle relevant information only for the shortest reasonable period necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. After that period, we will delete or stop processing such information in accordance with app functionality, system mechanisms, or your active actions.

## 6. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your personal information for commercial marketing purposes unrelated to the core functions of Auralis.

In the current product design, information processing mainly occurs in the following situations:

### 1. Necessary Cooperation with Apple Platform Capabilities

When you use features such as subscription purchases, purchase restoration, membership status verification, member resource downloads or caching, and photo library saving, related processing may rely on services provided by Apple.

### 2. Legal or Regulatory Requirements

Where required by applicable laws or regulations, or in response to lawful requests from judicial, administrative, or regulatory authorities, we may cooperate and provide necessary information to the extent permitted by law.

Except for the situations described above, we will not share, transfer, or publicly disclose your personal information without authorization.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You can manage your information and permissions in the following ways:

### 1. Manage Subscriptions

You can view, cancel, or manage your membership subscription through your Apple ID subscription management settings.

### 2. Manage Local Settings

You can change settings such as affirmation playback intervals, reading style, and dark mode within the app. You can also delete custom audio or custom backgrounds that you have imported. If you delete the app, related local data will usually be removed as well.

### 3. Manage System Permissions

You can adjust or disable Auralis's photo library permission at any time in iOS Settings.

## 8. Protection of Minors

If you are a minor, please read this Privacy Policy and decide whether to agree to it under the guidance of your parent or legal guardian. Parents and guardians should guide and manage minors' use of Auralis, including any subscriptions or purchases.

## 9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to product changes, legal or regulatory requirements, or business adjustments. The updated version will be made available through the app, linked pages, or other appropriate methods.

If an update materially affects your rights or obligations, we will take reasonable steps to bring it to your attention.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, how your personal information is handled, or your related rights, you may contact us through the contact information provided in the app.
